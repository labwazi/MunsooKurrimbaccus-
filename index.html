About Munsoo

Munsoo Kurrimbaccus is a prominent Mauritian figure known for his work in education, diplomacy, and social advocacy. He has served as the Vice-President of the Union of Private Secondary Education Employees (UPSEE), where he strongly defended the rights of teachers and pushed for fair reforms in private education. With decades of leadership experience, Munsoo is widely respected in academic and political circles.

In 2024, he was appointed as the High Commissioner of Mauritius to Pakistan, a testament to his diplomatic skills and commitment to representing Mauritius on the international stage. He is known for being outspoken, deeply patriotic, and committed to transparent governance and development.
